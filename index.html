THE PROBLEM
GREAT PACIFIC GARBAGE PATCH
Plastic pollution is no joke. Without proper regulation and cheap, nonsustrainable disposal methods, most plastic ends up in landfills or floating around our oceans. There's more unrecycled plastic affecting the ecosystem than you know.
STATISTICS
ARE YOU SURPRISED?
There are many "garbage patches" floating around in the ocean--the largest of which is millions of square miles in area. Most of the plastic breaks down into tiny pieces that are often consumed by oceanic life with great physical detriment.
A HOPELESS FUTURE
WHAT HAPPENS IF WE DO NOTHING?
The plastic pollution in our oceans has already endangered several species (and affects up to 300). Without quick thinking and a global effort to nip this issue in the bud, we risk the extinction of sea life and polluting our own food supply with toxic chemicals.
WHAT CAN WE DO?
MANY SOLUTIONS
All solutions dealing with plastic pollutions boil down to the classic three Rs: reduce, reuse, recycle. It is our belief that we must combat the problem at the issue and reduce the amount of plastic production and usage as much as possible, in order to reduce the amount of resultant waste.
OUR SOLUTION
HELP US HELP THE WORLD
We make it easy to make an impact by simply doing your daily chores. When you buy groceries or any other product, we help you pick the most sustainable options at reasonable costs. You can choose the tradeoffs between sustainability and cost/efficiency. It's all up to you!
